About Entrepreneurship Development Cell, RCCIIT

Welcome to the Entrepreneurship Development Cell of RCC Institute of Information Technology, Kolkata!

We strive to foster the Entrepreneurial ecosystem within the young entrepreneurs within our college as well as in other institutes. The cell has been organizing various events regarding start-up development and entrepreneurship in various fields of science and technology. We are also a perennial platform for the students to pitch their new and innovative ideas and get subsequent guidance for turning it into a successful venture!

The Workshops, Talks, and events organized by the cell are primarily targeted towards motivating students for having an entrepreneurial spirit in them, which we believe is a must-have in todayâ€™s competitive market.They are also a fantastic opportunity to connect with well-known mentors, investors, and entrepreneurs and get their precious advice regarding career and entrepreneurship!

The Cell was established in the year 2011 and has so far organized various such events and workshops which have received a fantastic response from the participants.

Students from our institute have gone on to become entrepreneurs and start their own ventures thus adding value to the economy and making the market much more diverse by their innovative ideas.

We hope to join you in your journey towards becoming a successful entrepreneur!
