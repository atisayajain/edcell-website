Upsurge - The Entrepreneurship Summit

Upsurge 2018 – The Entrepreneurship Summit of RCC Institute of Information Technology is a 3-day event involving talks from eminent entrepreneurs and investors, workshops on educating topics relating to entrepreneurship and startups, and competition to give the attendees a real to life experience of being an entrepreneur and being a part of startups.

The aim of the event is to motivate the students of the college to bring out their passion of entrepreneurship in whichever field they want so that they can disrupt their chosen field in a better way and become the leaders of the future.

We also aim to connect our students with the potential investors and entrepreneurs of the region with whom they can discuss their upcoming projects and ideas and get them validated.

Also, we introduce the freshers to the concept of entrepreneurship by engaging them in all the events and competitions and creating special events for them.
